About Us

At MYTHBEAT Real Records, we are driven by a passion for music and a commitment to nurturing artistic talent while also growing as a label. Founded in 2016, our label has been a hub for creativity and innovation. We believe in the power of music to inspire, connect, and go beyond boundaries.

Our mission at MYTHBEAT Real Records is to Make sure that the music industry is more open artists to be heard. Whether it's supporting emerging artists, pushing the boundaries of genres, or fostering a community of like-minded music enthusiasts, we are dedicated to creating an environment where musical expression flourishes.
